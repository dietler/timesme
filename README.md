An interactive math quiz game designed for iPad, focused on practicing multiplication and division with 7, 8, and 9 times tables.

## Features

- **20 Questions per Round**: Complete a full round of 20 multiplication and division problems
- **Focus on 7, 8, 9 Times Tables**: All problems involve multiplying or dividing 7, 8, or 9 by numbers 0-12
- **Interactive Answer Selection**: Choose from 4 colorful answer circles
- **Instant Feedback**: Visual and emoji feedback for correct/incorrect answers
- **Fun Animations**: Confetti, bounces, scales, and celebration effects
- **Answer Review**: Review all answers after completing a round
- **Statistics Tracking**: Track your performance across multiple rounds with persistent localStorage
- **iPad Optimized**: Responsive design specifically tailored for iPad screens

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Answer each math question by tapping one of the four colorful answer circles
3. Get instant feedback with animations and emoji
4. Complete all 20 questions to see your final score
5. Review your answers to see which ones you got right or wrong
6. View your statistics to see which problems you've mastered and which need more practice
7. Play again to improve your score!

## Statistics Features

- **Most Missed Problems**: See which math problems you struggle with most
- **Best Performance**: See which problems you've mastered
- **Overall Stats**: Track total questions, accuracy, and more
- **Persistent Data**: All statistics are saved locally in your browser

## Technical Details

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - no frameworks required
- localStorage for persistent statistics
- Responsive design with CSS animations
- Object-oriented JavaScript architecture

## Browser Compatibility

Works best on modern browsers, optimized for iPad Safari.
